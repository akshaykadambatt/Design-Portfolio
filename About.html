Hi! I am Deepali.

I am a graphic, web and visual artist. My Philosophy is that functionality always takes precedence over mere aesthetics. With a passion for Realism, Expressionism and Post-modern design, I approach each project with strong research, vibrant use of color, experimental typography, and handcraft. I love designing visual identities, editorial, packaging, illustration, and websites. I strive to transform the mundane into the visually striking and appealing.
